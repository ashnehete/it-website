Department Library

The department library strives to connects outstanding faculty and brilliant students to the knowledge and state of-the-art of their disciplines, and strengthens the aspirations of teaching work thereby supporting the institute’s march towards its vision.

List of books in Department Library

Issuing Mechanism for Faculties

  1. Faculty can issue two books at a time for a period of one month. No penalty/fine for returning late.
  2. At the end of the academic year every one has to return all the books for stock verification.
  3. Faculty’s no dues form is not signed by HOD unless he/she returns the departmental library books. This is verified with library incharge.
  4. One has to give copy of book if misplaced by him/her.

Issuing Mechanism for Students

  1. Students can issue only one book at a time for a period of two weeks. No penalty/fine for returning late. But if request comes for same book after two weeks, then he/she is forced to return the book.
  2. At the end of the academic year everyone has to return all the books for stock verification.
  3. Student’s no dues form is not signed by HOD unless he/she returns the departmental library books. This is verified with library incharge.
  4. One has to give copy of book if misplaced by him/her.
